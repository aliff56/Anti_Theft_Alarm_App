# 🔒 Anti-Theft Alarm App

A **Flutter-based security app** designed to protect your phone from theft or unauthorized access.  
The app uses motion and proximity sensors, alarms, and background services to alert you instantly if someone tries to move or steal your phone.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📱 Motion Sensing Mode
- Press **Activate** to start a **10-second countdown**, giving you time to place your phone.
- Once armed, if **anyone touches or moves the phone**, a loud alarm is triggered.

### 🧥 Pickpocket Mode
- Works with **motion + proximity sensors**.
- Alarm rings if the phone is **removed from your pocket**.
- Smart detection: no false alarms while the phone stays inside your pocket, even if you’re walking or moving.

### 🔔 Alarm & Alert Options
- Multiple **pre-built alarm sounds** to choose from.
- **Preview alarm sounds** before setting one.
- Option to **set alarm sound as ringtone**.
- Control the **volume level** at which the alarm plays.
- **Flashlight and vibration** alerts can be enabled for extra effect.

### ⚙️ Advanced Settings
- **Auto-close app** after alarm activates, for added security.
- **Auto-deactivate alarm** at a scheduled time if user prefers.
- Runs with a **robust background service**:
  - Works even if the app is fully closed.
  - Ensures reliable protection without interruptions.
